About

Built for Real
Job Seekers

SmartDocs-AI was built as a final-year AI project with one goal: give every student and career-switcher the same quality of interview prep that was once reserved for those who could afford a human coach.

The system runs on Botpress's no-code canvas with a built-in RAG pipeline over curated PDF question banks, evaluated against the STAR methodology and powered by GPT-4.
